Registration for the Brainhack Montreal 2023 is now open!

Brainhack Montreal 2023 is a local hub of Brainhack Global 2023. The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: December 8 & 11-12, 2023

Costs: 30$ - Breakfast and lunch will be provided.

Location: Montreal, QC, Canada

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to https://brainhack.org/global2023/projects/

Please join the Brainhack Mattermost for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
